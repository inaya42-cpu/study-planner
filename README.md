# 📚 Study Planner

An intuitive, interactive web application designed to help Class 11 students (and anyone looking to boost their productivity) track their subjects, log topics, and keep an eye on upcoming exam deadlines.

🔗 **[Live Demo](https://inaya42-cpu.github.io/study-planner/)**

---

## ✨ Features

*   **📅 Exam Countdown:** Dynamically calculates the days remaining until your Board Exam date.
*   **📊 Productivity Metrics:** Automatically tracks the number of required tasks per day to keep you on schedule.
*   **📈 Topic Progress Chart:** Visualizes your completed topics by subject using clean, interactive bar charts.
*   **🛠️ Custom Subject & Topic Management:** Add custom subjects (like Biology, Math) and pinpoint specific topics you need to work on.
*   **📌 Focus Mode:** Pin and highlight specific topics to emphasize your "Today's Focus".

---

## 🚀 Built With

*   **HTML5** - Structured layout
*   **CSS3** - Clean, responsive UI with a vibrant modern palette
*   **JavaScript (ES6+)** - Dynamic tracking logic, date calculations, and state management

---

## 🛠️ How to Use

1.  **Set Your Deadline:** Enter your Board Exam date in the settings panel to activate the countdown timer.
2.  **Add Your Subjects:** Input the subjects you are studying this term.
3.  **Log & Track Topics:** Add specific topics under each subject. Check them off as you complete them to see your progress chart update!
4.  **Stay Focused:** Use the "Focus" pin to highlight what you need to tackle right now.
